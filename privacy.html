Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Vera, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we ensure its security.

1.1. Our Commitment to Transparency

At Vera, we are dedicated to maintaining full transparency about how we handle your data. We ensure that our processes comply with the highest standards of security and privacy. Your trust is our priority, and we strive to make every interaction safe and secure.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information:

  • Personal Data: Such as your name, email address, and any details you share with us when you contact us or use our services.

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP addresses, browser types, and pages viewed.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services.

  • Respond to your inquiries and offer personalized support.

  • Analyze usage trends to enhance your experience.

  • Communicate important updates or promotional offers (only with your consent).

4. Protecting Your Data

We implement advanced security measures to safeguard your information:

  • Secure data encryption during transmission.

  • Regular monitoring for potential threats.

  • Restricted access to personal data, limited to authorized personnel.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update the personal data we hold about you.

  • Request the deletion of your data unless required for legal purposes.

  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
